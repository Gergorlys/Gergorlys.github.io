Alterations and Renovations

We have become specialists in this field over the years, with most of renovation work taking place on Leisure and Thesen Island. We have renovated many of the older houses on Leisure Island over the years and have developed a good understanding of the building and environmental conditions specific to that Island. We also do a lot of renovations on Thesen Island, many due to new owners altering the houses to suit their needs. Besides on these two islands, we have done many alterations in various locations, and enjoy the challenge of adding our own spin to pre-existing houses.
