From Experience

Experience has taught us that good design is timeless and a well-designed and built building is a pleasure to live in. We do not necessarily follow all the latest design and building material “fads” that come and go. The advice we give our clients on style, function, materials used, detailing etc. are based on experience and a detailed knowledge of the local building industry, climate, and materials. It is important that our buildings live well, are easy to maintain, cost effective to build and have a long-life span.
