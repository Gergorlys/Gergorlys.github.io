Who Are We? Our Philosophy
Brymer and associate’s architects based in Knysna on the Picturesque Garden Route in South Africa is a professional architect’s firm owned and run by husband-and-wife duo Ian and Merinda Brymer. The company has been operating in the Garden route for 30 years and specialises in domestic, light commercial and educational design and supervision. Most of our clients are constantly on the move and they generally do not have a lot of time to spend on the project. To facilitate their needs, we offer a turnkey service. Brymer and Associates design philosophy is geared towards the needs and desires of our clients, it is therefore very important to us that we provide our clients with an end product that suits their lifestyle and budget. Experience has given us the set of skills needed to interpret and develop a client’s brief, allowing us to deliver a building that suits our clients need in every way. Generally, our clients opt to use our services on multiple projects which bares testament to our ability to deliver a quality product.
Merinda Brymer
Ian Brymer
Merinda, who has a degree in architecture, is responsible for the initial client liaison, client brief and site analysis, the designing of drawings and municipal/development submissions. Merinda looks at design holistically with a strong emphasis on the clients needs and paring this to the site and local conditions. Our design drawings are detailed with a strong emphasis placed on interior design and details. This allows builders to accurately price our drawings and deliver a good product without costly changes to design. Ian, who has a BSc degree, is responsible for the architectural supervision and office management. Good management of the construction process is pivotal to delivering a good end product, and with their close working relationship and constant communication, Merinda and Ian ensure that the architect’s and client’s vision is accurately portrayed to the management team. Details like choosing the right builders for the project, specifying the correct materials, managing the installation of the technical elements, minimising drawing interpretation errors all go a long way to delivering a great building that will be well used for many years. Strong emphasis is also placed on finishing details like landscaping, interior design and detailing and helping with soft finishing and joinery. The quality of the project team is very important as a lot of members of the team will develop long term relationships with the building and client.
